About this compilation: This collection provides insights into how Block'hood has been received across different communities - from gaming media to academic institutions. The game, developed by Jose Sanchez and Plethora-Project, represents an innovative approach to city-building that focuses on ecological balance and interdependencies rather than traditional economic systems.
